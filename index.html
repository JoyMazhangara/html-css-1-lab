Everybody Always: Becoming Love in a World Full of Setbacks and Difficult People

By Bob Goff

In his entertaining and inspiring follow-up to the New York Times bestselling phenomenon "Love Does ,
Bob Goff takes readers on a journey into the secret of living without fear, contsraint, or worry. The path
toward the liberated existence we all long for is found in a truth as simple to say as it is hard to
do: love people, even the difficult ones, without distinction and without limits.

Driven by Bob's trademark storytelling, "Everybody, Always" revels the lessons Bob
learned--often the hard way--about what it means to love without inhibition, insecurity, or restriction.
From finding the right friends to discovering the upside of failure, "Everybody, Always" points the
way to embodying love by doing the unexpected, the intimidating, the seemingly impossible. Whether
losing his shoes while skydiving solo or befriending a Ugandan witch doctor, Bob steps into life with
a no-limits embrace of others that is as infectious as it is extraordinarily ordinary. "Everybody, Always"
reveals how we can do the same.
